This plugin allows you to easily embed Online surveys and forms built with Inqwise's online survey software into your sidebar, pages, and posts.

== Description ==

Allows the use of a special short code [inqwise] for embedding [Inqwise online surveys and forms](http://www.inqwise.com/?gid=5).

**Example:** 
`[inqwise guid="036b6bbd-949e-4669-b880-26fac57963fd"]`

== Installation ==

For old school manual installation people: Unzip "inqwise-shortcode.zip" file and copy the folder "inqwise-shortcode" into the /wp-content/plugins/ folder. Then go to the Plugins area of the Admin and activate. Otherwise, search for "Inqwise Shortcode" from the admin area of your WordPress site in Plugins > Add New.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

**What is Inqwise?**

[Inqwise](http://www.inqwise.com/?gid=5) is an online survey software and questionnaire tool that help you create online surveys and forms.

**What can I build with this?**

Basically, the possibilities are endless. Here's a short list of examples to give you an idea of Inqwise's versatility:

* Customer Satisfaction Survey
* Market Research Survey
* Business Survey
* Consumer Survey
* Meeting and Event Planning Survey
* Job Satisfaction Survey
* Employee Survey
* Product Feedback Survey
* HR Survey & Training Survey
* Non-Profit Surveys 
* Feedback Form
* Contact Form
* Mailing List
* Job Application
* Bug Tracker
* Invitations / RSVP
